M365 Services Responsibilities: Role Clarification for M365 Services and Help Desk¶

Purpose¶

This document aims to clearly delineate the responsibilities and support tasks assigned to M365/Exchange Administrators and the Help Desk within RCIT. Establishing these boundaries ensures efficiency, prevents overlap, and maintains a high standard of service for our users.

Exchange Administrator Responsibilities¶

The Exchange Administrator is primarily responsible for the management, maintenance, and operation of the organization's Microsoft Exchange environment. Their key responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

1. Exchange Server Management:¶

  • Ensuring the health and uptime of Exchange servers.
  • Performing server updates, patches, and upgrades.
  • Monitoring performance and managing server resources.

2. Exchange Online Management:¶

  • Overseeing the configuration and management of Exchange Online settings.
  • Managing cloud-based mailboxes, policies, and features unique to Exchange Online.
  • Management of hybrid deployment solution between Exchange on-premsies and Exchange Online.

3. Security and Compliance:¶

  • Implementing and monitoring security measures for email communications.
  • Managing mail flow and transport rules.
  • Ensuring compliance with data protection laws and policies.

4. Disaster Recovery and Business Continuity:¶

  • Developing and maintaining Exchange disaster recovery plans.
  • Testing backup and restoration procedures.

5. Advanced Troubleshooting:¶

  • Resolving complex issues escalated from the Help Desk.
  • Identifying and addressing root causes of recurrent problems.

6. Technical Leadership and Consultation:¶

  • Advising on best practices for email system usage and configuration.
  • Collaborating with other IT teams for integrated services.

M365 Administrator Responsibilities¶

The M365 Administrator oversees the organization's entire Microsoft 365 suite, ensuring seamless integration and operation of various M365 services. Key responsibilities include:

1. Service Administration:¶

  • Managing and configuring core M365 services, including but not limited to SharePoint Online, OneDrive for Business, and Teams.

2. Security and Compliance:¶

  • Implementing and managing security and compliance features within M365, such as Advanced Threat Protection, Data Loss Prevention, and compliance standards in Microsoft Teams.
  • Regularly reviewing and auditing M365 security and compliance settings.

3. Collaboration Tools Management:¶

  • Administering collaborative tools like Microsoft Teams, ensuring proper integration with other M365 services.
  • Assisting in the deployment and management of Teams voice, video, and meetings functionalities.

4. User Training and Adoption:¶

  • Facilitating the adoption of M365 services through user training and support documentation.
  • Providing updates and best practices for utilizing M365 services.

5. Reporting and Monitoring:¶

  • Monitoring the usage and performance of M365 services.
  • Generating reports on service utilization, audit logs, and compliance checks.

Help Desk Responsibilities¶

The Help Desk provides first-line support for both on-premises Exchange, Exchange Online, and M365 services. Responsibilities include:

1. User Account Management:¶

  • Managing user accounts for M365 and Exchange, including creating, modifying, and maintaining user accounts.

2. Basic Troubleshooting:¶

  • Assisting users with setup and configuration for Exchange (e.g., Outlook) and M365 services.
  • Resolving common connectivity and accessibility issues.
  • Guiding users through troubleshooting steps for email delivery issues.

3. User Guidance and Support:¶

  • Educating users on the features, security practices, and effective use of Exchange and M365 tools.
  • Handling queries related to email and collaborative services.

4. Ticket Management:¶

  • Recording, categorizing, and prioritizing user requests and issues.
  • Escalating complex issues to Messaging/M365 Service, or relevant RCIT teams.

5. Monitoring and Alerts:¶

  • Notifying users of maintenance schedules or system downtimes.
