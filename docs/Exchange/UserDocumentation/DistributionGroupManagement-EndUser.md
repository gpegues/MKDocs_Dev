# M365 Services - Modify Members of a Distribution List (OWA)

## Overview
This document walks you through navigating to the Distribution Groups area, viewing distribution group membership, and adding and removing members.

### Click on the links below to skip directly to a section:

- [To Remove Members from a Distribution List That You Own](#to-remove-members-from-a-distribution-list-that-you-own)

### To View Members of a List

1. Sign into Office 365 using a web browser. https://outlook.office365.com/ecp/MyGroups/PersonalGroups.aspx
2. From the app navigation waffle in the upper-left corner, select the Outlook app, if you are not already there.
3. On the Outlook ribbon, click on the Settings wheel button.
4. In the left navigation pane of the Settings page, click to expand the General selection list.
5. From the General list, select Distribution Groups.
6. In the text field, click the link that displaying "To manage distribution groups, visit this portal". As an alternative, you can skip prior steps by manually navigating to the following URL: https://outlook.office365.com/mail/options/general/distributionGroups  
7. In the "Distribution groups I own" section, select the distribution group you want to modify from the list and click the pencil icon. Please note, if the distribution list that you want to modify is not listed, you may not be the list owner or the list may be hidden.
8. On the Distribution Group window that opens, select membership from the left menu to view members of the list.

### To Add Members to a Distribution List That You Own

1. After following the instructions above to view group membership, click on the '+' (plus) button. A new window will open with the Office 365 People directory.
2. From the Search results, click on the button that corresponds to the person you want to add to the distribution list. The person will be displayed in the Members row. Repeat until all new members have been selected.
3. Once all new members have been selected and added to the Members row, click Save.
4. Verify that the new members have been added to the Members list in the Distribution Group window. If no further changes are necessary, click Save.
5. Click the close button in the upper right corner of the Settings page to return to your Office 365 mail.

### To Remove Members from a Distribution List That You Own

1. After following the instructions above to view group membership, click on the group member you want to remove from the list.
2. Click the - (minus) button.
3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 for all people you want to remove from the distribution list.
4. Verify that the Members list is updated. If no further changes are necessary, click Save.
5. Click the close button in the upper right corner of the Settings page to return to your Office 365 mail.
