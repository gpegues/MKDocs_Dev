### Pricing and Recommendations (Pricing as of 5/19/2025)

| License                          | Annual Cost                  | Included in G3 License  |
|---------------------------------|------------------------------|-------------------------|
| Power Apps Premium for Government | $210.74 per user              | ❌ No                    |
| Power Apps for Office 365 for Government (G3)    | Included                      | ✅ Yes                   |

**Recommendations:**

- **Power Apps Premium for Government:** Suitable for apps that require Dataverse, premium connectors, or complex workflows. Costs $210.74 annually per user.  
- **Power Apps for Office 365 for Government:** Included with G3 licensing. **Not enabled by default**. It can be activated through a service request with managerial approval and RCIT review. Recommended for basic apps using standard connectors (SharePoint, Teams).
