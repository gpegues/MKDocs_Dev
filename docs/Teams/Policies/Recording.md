### Teams Recording Policies

Teams recording has two different policies regarding recording. One being 1:1 and one being for meetings. As of 3/22/2024, we currently have 1:1 disabled, but meetings enabled. Below are where to find these two policies in the admin portal

#### 1:1 Recording Policy

- **Location**: Voice > Calling Policies
- **Recording Option**: Cloud recording for calling

#### Meeting Policy

- **Location**: Meetings > Meeting Policies
- **Recording & Transcription Option**: Meeting recording
